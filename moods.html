Mood Evaluation Guidlines

Insight breaks moods down into simple numerical values to make recording as easy as possible.

Rankings are based on a seven-point scale, where +3 represents the most positive feelings and -3 represents the most negative. Over time, you will associate each rating with your own individual moods.

Below is a general guideline to get you started.

  • +3Mania, Hypomania
  • +2Agitated, Elated, Euphoric
  • +1Happy, Content, Optimistic
  • 0Neutral, Normal
  • -1Sad, Bummed, Irritated
  • -2Depressed, Angry, Irate
  • -3Suicidal, Berserker-like Rage
